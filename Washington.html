Culture

Washington is known for its tech industry, coffee culture, and music history. Seattle gave rise to grunge music, and the state embraces innovation and outdoor living.

Lifestyle & Outdoors

Washington offers a mix of urban innovation and natural beauty. Hiking, kayaking, skiing, and enjoying local coffee shops are popular ways to experience the state.
